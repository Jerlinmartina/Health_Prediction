Health Risk Derivation

How the target variable is constructed for supervised learning.

Since the dataset has no explicit health risk label, we derive a composite risk score from four key indicators:

⚖️
BMI
<18.5 or >30 = risk
💓
Blood Pressure
Systolic >130 = risk
🩸
Cholesterol
>200 = risk
🍬
Blood Glucose
>100 = risk

Each indicator contributes 0–2 points. Total score ≤2 = Low Risk, 3–4 = Medium Risk, ≥5 = High Risk.
